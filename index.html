Dr. Xu Chi

I am a Professor with School of Automation, China University of Geosciences (CUG). Before I joined CUG I worked as a Senior Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Machine Learning for Bioimage Analysis Group, BII, A*STAR, Singapore.

Research Interests:

Computer Vision, Hand Pose Estimation, Camera Pose Estimation, Robotics, Augmented Reality, Machine Learning, etc.
